Welcome to the Confidence Through Evidence Toolkit

This toolkit has four steps designed to help you Acquire, Assess, Adapt and Apply evidence in your practice. Each step includes key questions and signposts to relevant supporting resources aimed at improving your skills and confidence in each of these areas.

More information about the toolkit is outlined in the Introduction.

You may be interested in our evidence-informed practice collection, which contains a range of free resources including Using and Doing Research, a series of three short modules. You may also find relevant our Insights series of evidence summaries and case studies highlighting how organisations are using evidence to inform practice.

We welcome your feedback, so please do get in touch our Evidence-informed Practice Team with your comments.
