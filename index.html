Confidence through evidence toolkit

Making better use of evidence in social services

This toolkit from Iriss has four steps designed to help you acquire, assess, adapt and apply evidence in your practice. Each step includes key questions and signposts to relevant supporting resources aimed at improving your skills and confidence in each of these areas.
